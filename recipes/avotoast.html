Avocado Toast

Is Avocado a veggie or fruit?

Enjoy this delicious toast as a snack or a meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole avocado - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 slices ciabatta bread or any brown bread of your choice
  • 6 slices tomato
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Mix together avocado and garlic salt in a small bowl; spread onto bread slices.
  2. Lay tomatoe slices over avocado on the bread and season with black pepper.
