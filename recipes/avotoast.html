Avocado Toast

Is avocado a fruit or a veggie ?

Enjoy this delicious toast !!

Ingredients

  • 1 whole avocado - peeled, pitted and smashed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 slices ciabatta bread or any brown bread
  • 6 slices tomato
  • 1 pinch ground black paper to taste

Directions

  1. Mix together avocado and garlic salt in a small bowl; spread onto bread slices.
  2. Lay tomato slices over avocado and season with black pepper
