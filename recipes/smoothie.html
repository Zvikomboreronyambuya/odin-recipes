Smoothie

All in one fruit blend?

This recipe is super versatile and you can change it depending on your dietary needs or personal preference. Have fun !!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh/frozen blueberries
  • 1/2 cup Greek Yogurt
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1/2 cup strawberries
  • 3 ice cubes

Directions

  1. Blend blueberries, yogurt, orange juice and strawberries together ina blender on low speed for 30 seconds
  2. Increase speed to high and blend until smooth, about 2 minutes.
  3. Add ice and blend on high until smooth, about a minute more.
