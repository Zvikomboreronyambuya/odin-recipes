Smoothie

All in one fruit blend ?

This recipe is super versatile, and you can change it depending on dietary needs or personal preference. Have fun with it!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • ½ cup Greek yogurt
  • ¼ cup orange juice
  • ½ cup strawberries
  • 3 ice cubes

Directions

  1. Blend blueberries, yogurt, orange juice and strawberries together in a blender on low speed for 30 seconds
  2. Increase speed to high and blend until smooth, about 2 minutes.
  3. Add ice and blend on high until smooth, about 1 minute more.
